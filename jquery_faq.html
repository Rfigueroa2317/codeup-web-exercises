10 faqs about national parks

American Discovery trail
Tied in first place for longest trail in the US at 6,800 miles.
Great Western Loop
Tied in first place for longest trail in the US. Also at 6,800 miles
New River Gorge trail
Newest addition to the US walking trail system. It's about 7 miles in length.
Acadia
Covering most of Mount Desert Islan and other coastal islands. It features the tallest mountain on the U.S east coast.
American Samoa
The southernmost national park in the U.S is on three Samoan islands. Home to around 900 species of fish
Arches
Features more than 2,000 natural sandstone arches. Located in Utah
Badlands
The Badlands are a collection of buttes, pinnacles, spires, and mixed-grass prairies. Located in South Dakota.
Big Bend
Named for the Rio Grande river along the U.S- Mexico Border
